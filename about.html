Beauty comes from within the soul of a person. It's the work of a barber and cosmetologist to draw it out. Salon suraliya is committed to excellent, down-to-earth and approachable customer service. Here, you’ll find a place where all people are equally valued. We want our clients to have a first-class experience from the moment they walk through our doors. Barbering and cosmetology involve art and science, and we are committed to the development of both. As such, we seek to unleash each team member’s fullest potential by working together to create a first-class experience for our clients. Recognizing that art and science can get messy, Salon suraliya also seeks and fosters relationships with partners to reduce the environmental imprint of our salon service.
